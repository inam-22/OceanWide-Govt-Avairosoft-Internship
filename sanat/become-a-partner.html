HOW TO BECOME A OCEAN WIDE SERVICES PARTNER AND TEAM MEMBER?

Process is very simple - first contact must come from your side. You should email us brief explanation about your needs and proposals. Your email should give us enough information to enable us define our interest in your proposal. We suggest you to identify one of three ffered options below and prepare your information accordingly. Every request delivered on our official email address info@ocean-wide.net will be answered back to confirm its acceptance and forwarded to one of our team members responsible for that specific field or market. Please read below options of cooperation. Important notice: In case you are to offer Crude oil or precious metal transaction we suggest you to download and read our working policy before engaging. (downtown)

Offer ProjectInvest With UsOption to Trade

When you decide to send us Project Offering please present us with small project description (basic presentation) and try to define us what kind of offering it is:

  • offer to sell us full project
  • apply for financial assistance
  • offer to manage project and assets within 72 working hours after your information is received we will answer back clearly stating our interest in your proposal. In case of positive answer back we will expect you to send us more information and documentation about project.

OWS GmbH as a part of Sorabia Group is one closed system of companies capable to function independently and that makes this way of partnering the most complex way. It takes more time and energy for approval compared to others. Considering that all parties interested in joint investment opportunities are more knowledgeable we expect your full professional approach in first contact. For full Due Diligence we will require full set of documents:

  • CIS and ID documentation
  • Registration documents
  • Banking documents
  • ATV approval (download)

This is a basic level of cooperation where you can offer to sell us some service or commodity, or request from us to purchase service or commodity under our control.

n both cases your informational e-mail should have just enough information to be declared as serious request or offer, to pass first Due Diligence and to get answer back. After first acceptance we will request or issue official offer and request KYC documentation from you to complete our Due Diligence.
